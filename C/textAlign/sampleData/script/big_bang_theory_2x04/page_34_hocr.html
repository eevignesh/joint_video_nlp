The Big Bang Theory #204 "The Griffin Equivalency" 34. September 15, 2008 FINAL DRAFT (II/E)

WOLOWITZ So, not a puppy.

SHELDON Please. Nothing so pedestrian. I wanted a griffin.

LEONARD A griffin?

SHELDON Yes, half eagle, half lion.

LEONARD And mythological.

SHELDON Irrelevant. I was studying recombinant DNA technology and I was confident I could create one, but my parents were unwilling to secure the necessary eagle eggs and lion semen. Of course, my sister got swimming lessons when she wanted them.

WOLOWITZ Sheldon, not that we don't all enjoy a good lion semen story, but what's your
